Galois Theory Presentation

I was really surprised when I learned that there does not exist a formula allowing one to find the roots of any fifth degree polynomial. This encouraged me to study Galois Theory to really understand why such a formula is impossible. I am currently working on a presentation accessible to preuniversity students. The presentation is currently only available in French but an English version is coming soon.
